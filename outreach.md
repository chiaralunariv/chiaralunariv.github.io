## Outreach

I'm committed to making cognitive neuroscience and sign language research accessible to the general public, and particularly to the Deaf and Hard-of-Hearing community.

- **[BCBL.SIGN](https://www.instagram.com/bcbl.sign/)** (January 2025 – December 2025) — Helping manage and create content in LSE (Spanish Sign Language) for the outreach Instagram account BCBL.SIGN, with the goal of making cognitive neuroscience accessible to the Deaf community. The account is still active and managed by other members of the Lab.
### Event organization

- **Pint of Science 2025**, Donostia-San Sebastián, Spain (19–21 May 2025) — Organizer and coordinator of sign language interpretation for the event's talks.
- **Pint of Science 2024**, Donostia-San Sebastián, Spain (13–15 May 2024) — Organizer and coordinator of sign language interpretation for the event's talks.
- **Brain Awareness Week 2020**, Donostia-San Sebastián, Spain (15–20 March 2020) — Member of the organizing team.

### Public talks

- **Pint of Science 2024**, Donostia-San Sebastián, Spain — Outreach talk: *"Manos que hablan: descubriendo la iconicidad en la Lengua de Signos Española."*
- **Brain Awareness Week 2020**, Donostia-San Sebastián, Spain — Outreach talk: *"¿Cómo se comunica una persona con problemas auditivos?"*
- **School visits to BCBL** (2019–2025) — Short outreach talks for students aged 6–16 on sign language and deafness research.
