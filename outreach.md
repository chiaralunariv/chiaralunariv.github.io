## Outreach

I'm committed to making cognitive neuroscience and sign language research accessible — particularly to the Deaf community, and to the general public more broadly.

### Ongoing

- **BCBL.SIGN** (January 2025 – present) — Helping manage and create content in LSE (Spanish Sign Language) for the outreach Instagram account BCBL.SIGN, with the goal of making cognitive neuroscience accessible to the Deaf community.

### Event organization

- **Pint of Science 2025**, Donostia-San Sebastián, Spain (19–21 May 2025) — Organizer and coordinator of sign language interpretation for the event's presentations.
- **Pint of Science 2024**, Donostia-San Sebastián, Spain (13–15 May 2024) — Coordinator of sign language interpretation for the event's presentations.
- **Brain Awareness Week 2020**, Donostia-San Sebastián, Spain (15–20 March 2020) — Member of the organizing team.

### Public talks

- **Pint of Science 2024**, Donostia-San Sebastián, Spain — Outreach talk: *"Manos que hablan: descubriendo la iconicidad en la Lengua de Signos Española."*
- **Brain Awareness Week 2020**, Donostia-San Sebastián, Spain — Outreach talk: *"¿Cómo se comunica una persona con problemas auditivos?"*
- **School visits to BCBL** (2019–2025) — Short outreach talks for students aged 6–16 on sign language and deafness research.
